Why?

I created this project because I saw a common problem among League of Legends Software: the existing orbwalkers were both expensive and usually not even that good.

To address this issue, I decided to develop an orbwalker that would be accessible to all players, without compromising on quality. I spent hours researching, developing and learning to create a solution that was both affordable and reliable.

The result of my work is a user-friendly orbwalker that provides smooth movement and accurate targeting. Even though this project isn't perfect, we are getting there!

What exactly is this?

The "Based Orbwalker" is a piece of software for ADC players. This software serves as a tool to automate and optimize the orbwalking process, giving players an edge in their gameplay. It allows you to seamlessly navigate the summoners rift while automatically attacking enemies, maximizing your damage output. With this software, you can expect precise and accurate targeting, seamless movement, and improved efficiency in your attacks. It has been designed with user-friendliness in mind, making it accessible even for those who are new to orbwalking techniques.
